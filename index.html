About Us

About Us

Insight Mantra is a leading data analytics company that empowers businesses across more than 30 industries. By integrating, aggregating, and analyzing diverse data types from multiple sources, Insight Mantra addresses ambitious needs at both department and enterprise levels. Their expertise lies in providing actionable analytics solutions tailored to enhance business performance, drive strategic insights, and respond to the complex challenges faced by modern organizations.

What we provide at InsightMantra:

  • Daily, weekly, or monthly reports — based on your workflow.
  • Custom dashboards for sales, stock, finance — or whatever you track.
  • And the best part —
    • You don’t need to install Power BI or buy any license.

How it is actually works:

  • First, you share your data with us after signing a simple NDA —
    That gives you complete assurance that your data is fully protected.
  • Then we process it and send you a dashboard link —
    no software, no login — just open it in your browser.
  • You can also share it with your team or clients —
    so everyone can see what’s working, and what needs action
